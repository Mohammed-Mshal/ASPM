School Tours General Information

Book your school tour! Students and teachers alike now have the amazing opportunity to leave the classroom and history books behind, and immerse themselves in never before seen history unfolding under one roof! Walk through the historic corridors of Emperors, Kings and Princes, or learn about the Civilizations that resided on Kuwaiti soil thousands of years ago. Better yet witness the birth of our nation unfold through countless testimonies of scholars and travelers who once traversed through the narrow streets and bazaars of AlQurain. Al Salam Palace Museum opens its doors for students ages 12-18.
