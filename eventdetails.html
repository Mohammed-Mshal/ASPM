Kuwait National Day Reception

Date: February 25, 2025
Time: 7:00 PM – 10:30 PM
Entry: Invitation Only (Official Dignitaries & Guests)

Event Overview

The Kuwait National Day Reception at Al Salam Palace is a prestigious annual event hosted by His Highness the Emir of Kuwait to celebrate the country’s independence. The evening includes diplomatic receptions, cultural performances, and a grand banquet honoring Kuwait’s heritage and achievements.

Event Schedule (Dummy Data)

Time Activity
7:00 PM Arrival of Guests & Official Welcoming
7:30 PM Speech by His Highness the Emir
8:00 PM Traditional Kuwaiti Music & Dance Performance
8:45 PM State Banquet & Dinner Reception
9:30 PM Fireworks Display (Palace Grounds)
10:30 PM Event Concludes

Featured Attendees (Dummy List)

  • His Highness Sheikh Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah
  • Kuwaiti Government Ministers & Officials
  • Foreign Ambassadors & Diplomats
  • Prominent Kuwaiti Business Leaders
  • Media Representatives (Approved Press Only)

Additional Information

  • Dress Code: Formal (National Dress / Business Attire)
  • Security: Strict screening – no personal cameras allowed
  • Parking: Designated areas for official vehicles only
  • Live Coverage: Kuwait TV (KTV1) will broadcast highlights

Note for the Public

  • This event is not open to the public.
  • Citizens can watch the ceremony live on national television.
