Standard Tour

Explore Kuwalt's rich history on an all-inclusive tour of Al Salam Palace Museum. Our expert guides will take you on a journey through the palace's story and the history of the land. It's the perfect way to experience Kuwait's past.

Things to consider before booking

  • Kids under the age of 12 are not allowed inside the Museum.
  • It is prohibited to bring food and drink into the Museum
  • There is a multi-story parking on the premises
  • There is a coffee shop at the main lobby
