Event Overview

The Kuwait National Day Reception at Al Salam Palace is a prestigious annual event hosted by His Highness the Emir of Kuwait to celebrate the country’s independence. The evening includes diplomatic receptions, cultural performances, and a grand banquet honoring Kuwait’s heritage and achievements.

Note for the Public

  • This event is not open to the public.
  • Citizens can watch the ceremony live on national television.
